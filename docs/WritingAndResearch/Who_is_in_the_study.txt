Ontario's 137 registered public hospitals do not all contribute equally to this analysis. Nine hospitals have no extractable strategic plan — four are blocked from automated access by their websites' robots protocols, and five have confirmed that no current plan exists. The blocked hospitals include Windsor Hotel Dieu Grace, Windsor Regional, Bluewater Health, and Chatham-Kent Health Alliance. Among the five with no plan: Waterloo Wellington Regional Hospital Network recently merged two legacy institutions and has not yet produced a combined strategic document; Toronto Casey House confirmed it does not maintain a formal strategic plan; and Manitouwadge Sante has not responded to two outreach attempts. Hornepayne Community is confirmed excluded after three email attempts produced no plan. The working analytical dataset is therefore 128 hospitals — 94% of the registry — with 589 extracted strategic directions ready for analysis.
