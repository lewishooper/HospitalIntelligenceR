Thirteen strategic directions across six hospitals are excluded from the thematic analysis. Three hospitals — Kemptville District Hospital, Fort Frances Riverside Health Care, and Dysart et al Haliburton Health Services — produced extractions rated as thin quality, meaning the API recovered some content but not enough to support reliable classification. Their directions are retained in the master file but treated as unclassifiable. A further three hospitals are excluded on access grounds: Sudbury St. Joseph's Continuing Care, and two additional facilities whose websites restrict automated access, have directions in the master file from manual capture but are held out of the classified dataset pending a formal access resolution. The analytical universe for thematic classification is therefore 124 hospitals and 562 directions.
